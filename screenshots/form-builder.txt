FORM BUILDER SCREENSHOT
========================

This screenshot shows the Form Builder interface of the Air Forms Builder application.

Features visible:
- Form Builder title at the top
- Dashboard button (left arrow) for navigation
- Form Details card with form name input (pre-filled with "shaik")
- Airtable Configuration card:
  * Base selection dropdown showing "Project tracker" selected
  * Table selection dropdown showing "Tasks, timelines, and assignees"
- Available Fields card with field selection options
- Clean, organized layout with white cards on dark background

The interface allows users to create forms by selecting Airtable bases,
tables, and specific fields to include in their forms.
